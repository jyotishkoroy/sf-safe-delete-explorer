# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

In the interest of fostering an open and respectful community, contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- showing respect for differing viewpoints and experiences
- giving and receiving constructive feedback
- focusing on what is best for the project and its users
- acting with empathy and professionalism

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- harassment, intimidation, or discrimination
- personal attacks, trolling, or insulting language
- publishing private information without permission
- any conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing these standards and may take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to unacceptable behavior.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces, including issues, pull requests, discussions, documentation, and related communication channels.

## Enforcement

To report unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainer listed in the repository profile. Reports will be reviewed promptly and handled with discretion.
